    >Historical Places of India</h1>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Taj Mahal, Agra</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/6347687/pexels-photo-6347687.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Taj Mahal">
    <p>The Taj Mahal is a magnificent marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Hampi, Karnataka</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/15267925/pexels-photo-15267925/free-photo-of-brown-concrete-temple-during-night-time.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Hampi">
    <p>Hampi is an ancient city in Karnataka that was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire. It is renowned for its well-preserved ruins, temples, and historic monuments, showcasing the architectural excellence of the Vijayanagara era.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Qutub Minar, Delhi</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/16892577/pexels-photo-16892577/free-photo-of-qutub-minar.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Qutub Minar">
    <p>The Qutub Minar is a towering minaret located in Delhi. It was built in the 12th century and is an excellent example of Indo-Islamic architecture. The complex also includes ancient ruins and the Iron Pillar, known for its rust-resistant composition.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Maharashtra</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/2563602/pexels-photo-2563602.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=600" alt="Ajanta and Ellora Caves">
    <p>The Ajanta and Ellora Caves are a series of ancient rock-cut caves located in Maharashtra. These caves are known for their intricate carvings and exquisite paintings, showcasing the rich cultural and artistic heritage of ancient India.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Red Fort, Delhi</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/13471509/pexels-photo-13471509.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Red Fort">
    <p>The Red Fort, also known as Lal Qila, is a historic fort complex in Delhi. It was constructed in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan and served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Mysore Palace, Karnataka</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/9882016/pexels-photo-9882016.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Mysore Palace">
    <p>The Mysore Palace is a grand royal palace located in Mysore, Karnataka. It is known for its stunning architecture, intricate artwork, and vibrant lighting during special occasions. The palace is a popular tourist attraction.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Khajuraho Temples, Madhya Pradesh</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/5246781/pexels-photo-5246781.jpeg" alt="Khajuraho Temples">
    <p>The Khajuraho Temples are a group of ancient Hindu and Jain temples in Madhya Pradesh. These temples are renowned for their elaborately carved sculptures, depicting various aspects of life, love, and spirituality.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="place">
    <h2>Victoria Memorial, Kolkata</h2>
    <img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/9610478/pexels-photo-9610478.jpeg?auto=compress&cs=tinysrgb&w=1260&h=750&dpr=1" alt="Victoria Memorial">
    <p>The Victoria Memorial is a majestic marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal. It was built in memory of Queen Victoria and showcases a blend of British and Mughal architectural styles. The memorial now serves as a museum and art gallery.</p>
